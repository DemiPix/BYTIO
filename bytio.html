BYTIO

Hardware Youth Tech International Organization

A social club established at Tesoro High School to help teach peers about computer hardware and how each individual part works.

During July of 2022, Shyel Singh and Tyler Le decided they wanted to find a way to bring technology to everyone, starting at their own school, Tesoro High School. This would be done through a club they would form called BYTIO in which people could sign up and learn about the different parts of a computer, how one would know which one to buy, how it works with the other parts, and how to put it in their very own computer.
